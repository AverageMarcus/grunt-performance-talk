Optimize Web Fonts

Use Font Squirrel Webfont Generator to shrink down your fonts by removing glyphs you don't use (e.g. Asian characters, rare punctuation).

Alternatively, if using Google Fonts you can pass in ?text=abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz to get a subset font.

I have yet to find a nice way of automating this with Grunt and local fonts (other that .ttf). Would love to hears any suggestions
